Two new preprints: [LLM2Vec-Gen](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10913) proposes a self-supervised method that produces embeddings directly in an LLM's output space, and [The Illusion of Superposition?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06374) investigates whether language models actually leverage superposition when reasoning with latent chain-of-thoughts.
