Two new preprints: [LatentLens](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.00462) introduces a method for interpreting visual token representations in vision-language models, and [Operationalising the Superficial Alignment Hypothesis](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15829) proposes task complexity as a formal metric to study how "superficial" adaptation is.
